What is being Digitized? Why? Benefits and Harms For Better or Worse?
-The tattoos of the NBA players are being digitized into video games.
-The information or in this case the tattoos are being used to create video games.		 -The goal or purpose of digitizing the tattoos of the NBA players is to get quick recognition of that particular video game because of the popularity of the players. 😅 -The benefit is the video game will gain popularity fast because it is supported by a famous NBA player’s tattoo.
😔 -The harm in digitizing this is that it is subject to copyright and therefore, the video game can get sued by the players or their tattoo artist.		 -Based on this article, it is fair to say that the world is worse due to digital representation because this is a case of copyright since the NBA players or their tattoo artists didn’t give consent to let their tattoo be the face of a video game nor were there any credits given to them.
